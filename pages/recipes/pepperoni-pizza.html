Pepperoni Pizza Recipe

Ah yes, the ultimate lazy person pizza

Pizza is the cornerstone of every healthy meal (well not quite)!

Pepperoni pizza is my favourite. I rarely make pizza but when I do, I make sure it's delicious!

Ingredients:

  • 16 ounces pizza dough
  • 18 to 20 slices of pepperoni
  • 12 ounces of mozzarella cheese, grated
  • 1 teaspoon fresh oregano
  • Flour for rolling and shaping dough

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 500 degrees
  2. Make the sauce
  3. Roll out the dough
  4. Add the toppings
  5. Cook the pizza
  6. Slice and serve
