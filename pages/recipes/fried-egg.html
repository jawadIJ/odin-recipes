Fried Egg Recipe

Description

Eggs are vital for humans (well, almost vital).

Eggs can be cooked in various ways but my personal favourite is to fry them!

Ingredients:
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1 teaspoon salt and pepper
Steps:
  1. Preheat pan
  2. Cook the butter
  3. Crack open the eggs and place them in pan
  4. Salt and pepper to season
  5. Serve on bread
