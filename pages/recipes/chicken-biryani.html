Chicken Biryani Recipe

Chicken Biryani is dope af and white people are missin' out

Hyderabadi chicken biryani is an aromatic, mouth watering and authentic Indian dish with succulent chicken in layers of fluffy rice, fragrant spices and fried onions.

It is easier than most recipes while retaining the original taste and presented step by step. Cook like a native but with more ease!

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 2 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 tablespoon ghee
  • 1.5 lb chicken with bone
  • 1 teaspoon chilli powder

Steps:

  1. Slice onions very finely and fry until crisp
  2. Combine chicken wit all marinade ingredients
  3. Rinse basmati rice until clear
  4. Transfer chicken with marinade to a cooking pot
  5. Drain and spread rice over chicken
  6. Layer all cooked rice
  7. Cover with parchment paper, followed by aluminium oil
  8. Cool for five minutes before serving
